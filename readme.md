## 📌 Executive Summary
This repository contains the complete thesis and research framework for a comprehensive approach to company valuation. Moving beyond traditional Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) models, this work integrates **Macroeconomic Theory**, **Geopolitical Risk Analysis**, **Institutional Economics**, and **Technological Infrastructure** into a unified model.

This research serves as the theoretical and mathematical foundation for [**ValuaScript**](https://github.com/Alessio2704/valuascript-toolchain), a proprietary Domain-Specific Language (DSL) and C++20 compiler designed for high-performance financial simulations.

---

## 🔬 Core Research Pillars

### 1. Geopolitical & Institutional Integration
*   Application of the **Acemoglu-Johnson-Robinson (AJR) Framework** to determine Country Risk Premiums.
*   Modeling the impact of **Institutional Quality** on long-term cash flow sustainability.
*   Analysis of supply chain shocks and trade-war dynamics on valuation multiples.

### 2. Technological Transition Analysis (CISC vs. RISC)
*   A deep dive into the **Semiconductor Market**, specifically the transition from **x86 (CISC)** to **ARM (RISC)** architectures.
*   Technoeconomic modeling of how Instruction Set Architecture (ISA) shifts impact the valuation of global tech titans (Nvidia, Apple, Huawei, AWS).

### 3. Algorithmic Valuation Logic
*   Standardization of complex adjustments: R&D Capitalization, Operating Leases, and Convertible Securities.
*   Probabilistic modeling of distressed assets and cyclical industry volatility.
